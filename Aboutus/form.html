Our Aim

Our main purpose behind the creation of SCCI since the very beginning, which was bridging the gap between the technical life and the practical life in the marketplace. SCCI provides its participants with the technical skills and soft skills required in the form of sessions given throughout the year.
SCCI will run as a set of workshops concluded by a three-day conference. The workshops will span eight to nine weeks (once a week) to give student trainees (Participants) the chance to digest information and for student instructors to prepare instructional material.
